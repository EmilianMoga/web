ABOUT US

This started in 1996 with Air in the Paragraph Line, a zine produced by Jon Konrath as a newsletter to document
his writing exploits and mail to his friends. After six issues and an extended hiatus, the format expanded in 2005 to a small
literary journal of writing by Konrath and several other writers. He published work he enjoyed reading, the kind of fiction and
fact that kept him glued to the pages, in hopes that others would like it, too.
